1 Wives, submit - Or be subject to. It is properly a military term, alluding to that entire submission that soldiers pay to their general. (Eph 5:22), &c.
2 Be not bitter - (Which may be without any appearance of anger) either in word or spirit.
4 Lest they be discouraged - Which may occasion their turning either desperate or stupid.
5 Eyeservice - Being more diligent under their eye than at other times. Singleness of heart - A simple intention of doing right, without looking any farther. Fearing God - That is, acting from this principle.
6 Heartily - Cheerfully, diligently. Menpleasers are soon dejected and made angry: the single - hearted are never displeased or disappointed; because they have another aim, which the good or evil treatment of those they serve cannot disappoint.
10 Just - According to your contract. Equitable - Even beyond the letter of your contract.
