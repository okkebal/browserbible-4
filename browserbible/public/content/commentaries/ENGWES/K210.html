12 Full moon - That dominion seldom lasts long, which is founded in blood and falsehood.
29 Twentieth year - The meaning is, that he began his reign in the twentieth year after the beginning of Jotham's reign; or, which is the same thing, in the fourth year of Ahaz, son of Jotham.
32 To reign - Alone: for he had reigned before this, as his father's deputy.
34 Gate - Not of the temple, but of one of the courts of the temple, probably that which led to the king's palace.
