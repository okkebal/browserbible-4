2 Fastened it - To the wall or pillar.
4 Thou - Thou Israel, whom I took to myself, and brought hither in the loins of thy father Abraham, from a remote country. Called thee - From the midst of many great persons among whom he lived in Chaldea. Chosen - I have chosen thee and thy seed through all generations.
6 Confounded - Because the mischief which they contrived against thee shall fall upon themselves.
8 Will hold - Will enable thee to vanquish all thine enemies.
9 Thou worm - Who art weak in thyself, and trodden under foot by thy proud enemies.
10 An instrument - Such as were usual in those times and places. The mountains - The great and lofty potentates of the world.
11 Fan - When thou hast beaten them as small as chaff. In the Holy One - For to him, thou shalt ascribe thy victory.
