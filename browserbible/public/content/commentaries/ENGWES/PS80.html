1 Early - Speedily; as soon as I am seated in the throne.
2 An hearth - An hearth is heated or burnt by the coals which are laid upon it.
4 Skin - My flesh being quite consumed.
5 A pelican - Is a solitary and mournful bird.
8 Bread - The sense is, dust and ashes are as familiar to me as the eating of my bread; I cover my head with them; I sit, yea, lie down in them, as mourners often did.
9 Lifted me - As a man lifts up a thing as high as he can, that he may cast it to the ground with greater force.
11 Remembrance - Thy name, Jehovah, which is called by this very word, God's remembrance, or memorial, and that unto all generations, (Exod 3:15).
12 The set time - The end of those seventy years which thou hast fixed.
17 This - This wonderful deliverance shall be carefully recorded by thy people.
18 Looked - From heaven.
19 To loose - To release his poor captives out of Babylon, and from the chains of sin and eternal destruction.
20 To declare - That they might publish the name and praises of God in his church.
