3 In holiness - God is holy and just, and true in all his works.
6 Afraid - And stood still, as men astonished, do.
7 Poured - When the Israelites passed over the sea. Arrows - Hail - stones or lightnings.
9 Not known - Because the water returned and covered them.
10 Leddest - First through the sea, and afterwards through the wilderness, with singular care and tenderness, as a shepherd doth his sheep.
12 My law - The doctrine which I am about to deliver.
13 Parable - Weighty sentences. Dark sayings - Not that the words are hard to be understood, but the things, God's transcendent goodness, their unparallel'd ingratitude; and their stupid ignorance and insensibleness, under such excellent teachings of God's word and works, are prodigious and hard to be believed. Of old - Of things done in ancient times.
16 Established - This is justly put in first place, as the chief of all his mercies. A testimony - His law, called a testimony, because it is a witness between God and men, declaring the duties which God expects from man, and the blessings which man may expect from God.
