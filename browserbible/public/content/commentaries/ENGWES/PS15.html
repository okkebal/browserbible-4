1 The Lord - Thus he passes from the work of creation, to the works of providence, and from the instances of his power, in senseless and irrational creatures, to his power in over - ruling the thoughts and wills, and actions of men, whether single or united.
2 The Counsel - All his purposes and designs are always successful.
4 All men - Although he hath a relation to Israel, yet he hath a general care over all mankind, all whose hearts and ways he observes.
