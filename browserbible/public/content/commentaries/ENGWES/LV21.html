2 To go a whoring - To seek counsel or help from them.
4 Who sanctify you - Who separate you from all nations, and from their impurities and idolatries, to be a peculiar people to myself; and who give you my grace to keep my statutes.
5 Curseth - This is not here meant of every perverse expression, but of bitter reproaches or imprecations. His blood shall be upon him - He is guilty of his own death: he deserves to die for so unnatural a crime.
8 Confusion - By perverting the order which God hath appointed, and making the same off - spring both his own child and his grandchild.
9 Put to death - Except the one party was forced by the other. See (Deu 22:25).
10 They - All who consented to it.
11 Slay the beast - Partly for the prevention of monstrous births, partly to blot out the memory of so loathsome a crime.
13 See her nakedness - In this and several of the following verses, uncovering nakedness plainly appears to mean not marriage, but fornication or adultery.
16 They shall die childless - Either shall be speedily cut off ere they can have a child by that incestuous conjunction; if this seem a less crime than most of the former incestuous mixtures, and therefore the magistrate forbear to punish it with death; yet they shall either have no children from such an unlawful bed, or their children shall die before them.
17 His brother's wife - Except in the case allowed by God, (Deu 25:5).
