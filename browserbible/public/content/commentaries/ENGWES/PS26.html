2 As - As a sword, which cutteth the very bones, so painful are their reproaches.
5 Nation - So he calls the company of his enemies for their great numbers. Man - Probably Achitophel or Absalom.
7 Send out - That is, actually discover them. Truth - Thy favour, or the light of thy countenance, and the truth of thy promises made to me; or the true - light, the illumination of thy spirit, and the direction of thy gracious providence, whereby I may be led in the right way, to thy holy hill. Hill - Of Zion, the place of God's presence and worship.
