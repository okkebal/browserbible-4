1 He looketh - This is a farther illustration of God's powerful providence: as when he affords his favour to creatures, they live and thrive, so on the contrary, one angry look or touch of his upon the hills or earth, makes them tremble and smoke, as Sinai did when God appeared in it.
4 Praise ye the Lord - Heb. Hallelujah. This is the first time that this word occurs. And it comes in here on occasion of the destruction of the wicked. And the last time it occurs, (Rev 19:1),3,4,6, it is on a like occasion, the destruction of Babylon.

8 Glory - Glory in the God whom you serve, as the only true God.
9 Seek - The Lord in his strength, in his sanctuary, or before the ark, which is called God's strength. Face - His gracious presence.
10 Judgments - The punishments which he brought upon Egypt by his mere word.
11 Of Jacob - The only branch of Abraham's seed to whom the following blessings belong.
12 Judgments - God executes his judgments upon all nations and people.
13 Remembered - So as to perform it. The word - The promise. Commanded - Established. Thousand generations - To all generations.
14 Oath - Wherewith he ratified the covenant with Isaac, (Gen 26:3).
