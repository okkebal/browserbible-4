3 Towards - Towards the holy of holies, because there the ark was; from whence God gave oracular answers to his people.
4 Draw not - Do not drag me; as thou dost these, to execution and destruction.
6 Regard not - The providential works of God towards his people.
8 I am helped - He speaks of it as past, because God assured him by his spirit, that he had heard and accepted his prayers.
