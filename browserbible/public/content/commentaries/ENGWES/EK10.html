3 Judge - The question is doubled, to awaken the prophet more fully, and to quicken him to his work.
4 Her time - The time of ripeness in her sins, and of execution of judgments on her. To defile - For this does more defile them, and provoke God to wrath against them.
5 Thy days - The days of thy sorrows, and punishment. Art come - Thou art grown up to the eldest years in sin, beyond which thou art not to go.
6 Much vexed - Afflicted, impoverished, and ruined.
7 Every one - Not one to be found of a more merciful temper. To their power - According to their ability.
8 In thee - In Jerusalem.
9 Thou - O Jerusalem. Mine holy things - All mine institutions, temple, sacrifices, feasts.
10 Carry tales - Informers, or persons that for money, give in false witness against the innocent. They eat - Offer sacrifice on the mountains and feast there, in honour of their idols.
11 Discovered - Defiled their fathers bed.
14 Smitten mine hand - In testimony of my abhorrence.
15 Endure - Withstand the evils that are coming, or bear them when come.
17 In thyself - Whereas I was thine inheritance so long as thou wert a holy, obedient people; now be an inheritance to thyself, if thou canst.
19 Dross - Utterly degenerate, and base metal. The furnace - The afflictions I have laid upon them have not bettered them. The dross - While they loved mercy, did justly, walked humbly with their God, they were as silver; now they are but dross.
20 Gather you - From all parts. I will, by a secret over - ruling providence, bring you into Jerusalem, as into a furnace, where you may be consumed.
