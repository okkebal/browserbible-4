2 Ethan - Called also Jeduthun, chap.(9:16 2Chron 35:15), and in the titles of divers psalms.
12 Castles - So called, not only because, walled and well guarded by the country; but because they and their possessions were in a particular manner the care of divine providence. As God was their portion, so God was their protector. And a cottage will be a castle to those that abide under the shadow of the Almighty. The lot - Or, the first lot.
24 Coasts - Or, of their borders, of their country contained within its borders.
