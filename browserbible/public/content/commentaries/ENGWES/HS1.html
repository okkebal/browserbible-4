1 By a thousand - That sent out one thousand soldiers. An hundred - Shall lose nine parts of them.
2 Ye shall live - It shall be well with you.
3 Seek not - Consult not, worship not the idol at Bethel, Gilgal, or Beersheba.
4 The house of Joseph - The kingdom of the ten tribes, the chief whereof was Ephraim, the son of Joseph. In Beth - el - If once this fire breaks out, all your idols in Beth - el shall not be able to quench it.
6 The seven stars - A constellation, whose rising about September was usually accompanied with sweet showers. Orion - Which arising about November brings usually cold, rains and frosts intermixt very seasonable for the earth. The shadow of the earth - The greatest adversity into as great prosperity. Dark with might - Changes prosperity into adversity. That calleth - Commands the vapour to ascend, which he turns into rain; and then pours from the clouds to make the earth fruitful.
7 The strong - The mighty, victorious and insolent. Shall come - Shall rally and form a siege against their besiegers.
8 They - The judges and people. In the gate - Where judges sat, and where the prophets many times delivered their message.
9 Your treading - You utterly oppress the helpless. Ye take - Ye extort from the poor great quantities of wheat, on which he should live.
10 In the gate - In their courts of justice.
11 Shall keep silence - Be forced to it. Evil - Both for the sinfulness of it, and for the troubles, wars, and captivity now at hand.
