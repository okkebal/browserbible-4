1 Israel - If thou wilt not help me, yet spare thy people who suffer for my sake, and in my sufferings.
3 Trusted - I have committed my cause and affairs to thee.
5 For - I dare appeal to thee, because thou knowest I have a deep sense of thy loving - kindness, by which I have been led to love and obey thee.
6 Sat - Continued with them. I have been so far from an imitation of their wicked courses, that I have avoided their company. Vain - With false and deceitful persons. Go in - Into their company.
8 Compass - Approach to thine altar with my sacrifices: which I could not do with any comfort, if I were conscious of those crimes, whereof mine enemies accuse me.
10 House - Thy sanctuary and worship. Honour - Thy glorious and gracious presence.
