2 So - Unserviceable and vexatious.
4 Gladness - Shall be accomplished, and turned into enjoyment.
5 The way - The way of God's precepts. Strength - Gives strength, support, and protection.
6 Never be removed - They shall live happily here, and eternally in heaven. The earth - They shall not have so much as a quiet abode upon earth; much less shall they have any possession in heaven.
8 The lips - Knowledge is here ascribed to the lips, as it is to the hands, (Psal 78:72), because they are conducted by knowledge and wisdom. Acceptable - What is truly worthy of acceptation.
10 A false balance - The use of all false weights and measures in commerce.
