20 Nations - The neighbouring nations, Philistines, Syrians, Ammonites, Moabites, who were stirred up, by the overthrows which David had given some of them, by their jealousy at his growing greatness, and by their hatred against the true religion.
21 Yea - The repetition implies their frequency and fervency in this action.
22 Bees - ln great numbers. Thorns - Which burns fiercely, but quickly spends itself.
23 Thou - O mine enemy. The singular word is here put collectively for all his enemies.
24 Salvation - My Saviour.
25 Doth valiantly - These are the words of that song of praise now mentioned.
26 Exalted - Hath appeared evidently, and wrought powerfully and gloriously.
