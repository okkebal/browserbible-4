1 Harp, &c. - These instruments were used in the publick worship of God in the tabernacle.
2 A new song - Renewed or continued from day to day.
3 The word - All God's counsels and commands are wise, and just, and good. His works - All his works of providence agree with his word, and are the accomplishment of his promises or threatenings.
4 Goodness - He not only doth no man wrong, but he is kind and merciful to all men.
5 The word - God made this admirable structure of the heavens, and all its glorious stars; not with great pains and time, but with one single word. Host - The angels: or the stars.
6 Store - houses - Either in the clouds, or in the bowels of the earth.
