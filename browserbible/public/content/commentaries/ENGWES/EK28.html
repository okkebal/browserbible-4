23 The keepers - While, according to their courses, they had the charge of the house of God, and attended on the service of it.
24 The keepers - To preserve the fire perpetually on the altar.
26 The breadth - The whole breadth was eleven cubits, but the breadth of each leaf of this folding - gate was three cubits, and they met, or shut on an upright post, set in the middle of the gate space, and this was one cubit broad.
