1 Deceit - He who uses himself to lying in his common talk, will use falsehood and deceit in judgment.
2 Health - Tends to the comfort and benefit of others.
3 A moment - Liars, though they may make a fair shew for a season, yet are quickly convicted.
4 Deceit - They whose hearts devise mischief shall be deceived in their hopes, and bring trouble upon themselves: but they who by good counsels labour to promote peace, shall reap the comfort of it themselves.
