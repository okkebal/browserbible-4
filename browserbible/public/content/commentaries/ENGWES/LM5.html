1 Coveredst - Didst clothe the images thou hadst made. Set mine oil - In lamps to burn before them.
2 For a sweet savour - To gain the favour of the idol. Thus it was - All which is undeniable.
3 And those - These very children of mine hast thou destroyed. Sacrificed - Not only consecrating them to be priests to dumb idols; but even burning them in sacrifice to Molech. Devoured - Consumed to ashes. Is this - Were thy whoredoms a small matter, that thou hast proceeded to this unnatural cruelty?
4 For them - For the idols.
7 In every street - Idol temples were in every street; both in Jerusalem and her cities.
8 At every head of the way - Not content with what was done in the city, she built her idol temples in the country, wherever it was likely passengers would come.
9 Great of flesh - Naturally of a big, make, and men of great stature.
13 How weak - Unstable, like water. An imperious woman - A woman, that knows no superior, nor will be neither guided nor governed.
14 Not as an harlot - Common harlots make gain of their looseness, and live by that gain; thou dost worse, thou lavishest out thy credit, wealth, and all, to maintain thine adulterers.
17 Contrary - Here we may see, what the nature of men is, when God leaves them to themselves: yea, tho' they have the greatest advantage, to be better, and to do better.
21 Blood - Thou gavest the blood of thy children to idols in sacrifice; I will give thee thine own blood to drink.
