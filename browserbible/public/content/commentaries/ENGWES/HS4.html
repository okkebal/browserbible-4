1 It came to pass - In the vision. By whom - How shall any of Jacob escape, if thou dost cast him down?
2 Repented - This is spoken after the manner of men.
3 Shewed - In vision. Called - Commanded fire from heaven. A part - Of the land too.
6 By a plumb - line - Strongly and beautifully built.
7 Set a plumb - line - I will exactly measure the whole ten tribes. Pass by them - I will no more forbear, but will pull down all that is faulty.
8 The high places - The temples on high mountains built to idols. Of Isaac - The seed of Isaac.
9 In the midst - Openly, and publickly, endeavouring to stir up Israel to sedition or rebellion. The land - The people cannot bear all his harsh predictions.
10 Jeroboam shall die - He nowhere said so, but spake of his house distinguished from his person, as ver.(9).
12 The king's court - To which therefore thou oughtest to shew more respect, and not thus to affront the king in his own house.
13 No prophet - Not originally, or by study, or by any human designation. An herdman - By breeding and occupation I was and still am, an herdman. Sycamore fruit - This fruit was good food for man, or cattle.
15 The house of Isaac - The posterity of Isaac.
16 In a polluted land - Among the Heathen. Israel - The ten tribes.
18 In a polluted land - Among the Heathen. Israel - The ten tribes.
19 The end - Of God's patience towards Israel, the end of their ripening, they are now fully ripe, fit to be gathered. Pass by them - God had with admirable patience spared, but now he will no more pardon or spare.
