1 To subvert - Here are three things mentioned, which God approveth not.
2 Who - Nothing comes to pass in the world, but by the disposal of divine providence. This seems to be spoken in the name of the people of God, arguing themselves into a quiet submission, to their afflictions, from the consideration of the hand of God in them.
3 Evil - Doth not evil or trouble come out of God's mouth from his direction, and providence, as well as good?
4 Wherefore - The Jews, check themselves in their complaints from the consideration, that nothing had befallen them, but what was the just reward of their sins.
7 Thou - Thou hast plagued us according to the just desert of our sins.
14 Mine eye - The prophet speaks this of himself.
18 Dungeon - Dungeon seems here to be taken for the lowest condition of misery.
19 Cut off - I am undone, there is no hope for me.
21 Heard - In former afflictions. Hide not - Shew me now the same favour.
23 O Lord - Thou hast been wont to take my part against my enemies.
25 Seen - Thou hast been a witness to all their fury.
28 I am - At feasts, and at their merry meetings, I am all the subject of their discourse.
