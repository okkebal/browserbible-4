23 A row of building - A range of building on the inside of the walls of the lesser courts. Four - Four courts in the four corners.

27 Out - Of the inmost court. The outer gate - The outmost north - gate in the wall that compassed the whole mountain of the Lord's house.
28 He measured - By the line in his hand. He brought me - Went before, and the prophet followed; all this was in vision.
