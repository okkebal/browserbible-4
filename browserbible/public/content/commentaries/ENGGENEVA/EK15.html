1 Therefore thus saith the Lord GOD; Behold, I will stretch out my hand upon the Philistines, and I will cut off the {f} Cherethims, and destroy the remnant of the sea coast. (f) Which were certain garrisons of Philistines by which they often molested the Jews, of the Cherethims David also had a guard, (2Sa 8:18).
4 And it came to pass in the {a} eleventh year, in the first [day] of the month, [that] the word of the LORD came to me, saying, (a) Either of the captivity of Jeconiah or of the reign of Zedekiah.
5 Son of man, because that Tyre hath said against Jerusalem, Aha, she is broken [that was] the {b} gates of the people: she is turned to me: I shall be {c} replenished, [now] she is laid waste: (b) That is, the famous city Jerusalem to which all people resorted. (c) My riches and fame will increase: thus the wicked rejoice at their fall by whom they may have any profit or advantage.
