2 Take away the {f} dross from the silver, and there shall come forth a vessel for the refiner. (f) When vice is removed from a king, he is a meet vessel for the Lord's use.
3 Take {g} away the wicked [from] before the king, and his throne shall be established in righteousness. (g) It is not enough that he is pure himself, but that he put away others who are corrupted.

12 Whoever boasteth himself of a false gift [is like] {k} clouds and wind without rain. (k) Which have an outward appearance, and are nothing within.
