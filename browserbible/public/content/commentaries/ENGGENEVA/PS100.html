3 I {a} love the LORD, because he hath heard my voice [and] my supplications. (a) He grants that no pleasure is so great as to feel God's help in our need, neither that anything more stirs up our love toward him.
4 Because he hath inclined his ear unto me, therefore will I call upon [him] {b} as long as I live. (b) That is in convenient time to seek help, which was when he was in distress.
