2 Let {f} the extortioner catch all that he hath; and let the strangers spoil his labour. (f) He declares that the curse of God lies on the extortioners, who thinking to enrich their children by their unlawfully gotten goods, are by God's just judgment deprived of all.
5 {g} Let the iniquity of his fathers be remembered with the LORD; and let not the sin of his mother be blotted out. (g) Thus the Lord punishes to the third and fourth generation the wickedness of the parents in their wicked children.
