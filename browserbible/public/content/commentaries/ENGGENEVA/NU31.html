49 Command the children of Israel, that they give unto the {a} Levites of the inheritance of their possession {b} cities to dwell in; and ye shall give [also] unto the Levites suburbs for the cities round about them. (a) Because they had no inheritance assigned them in the land of Canaan. (b) God would have them scattered through all the land, because the people might be preserved by them in the obedience of God and his Law.
52 And ye shall measure from without the city on the east side {c} two thousand cubits, and on the south side two thousand cubits, and on the west side two thousand cubits, and on the north side two thousand cubits; and the city [shall be] in the midst: this shall be to them the suburbs of the cities. (c) So that in all were three thousand, and in the compass of these two thousand, they might plant and sow.
