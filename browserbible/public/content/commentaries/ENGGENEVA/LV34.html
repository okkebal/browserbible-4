5 These [were] the renowned of the congregation, {e} princes of the tribes of their fathers, heads of thousands in Israel. (e) Or captains, and governors.
7 And they assembled all the congregation together on the first [day] of the second month, and they declared {f} their pedigrees after their families, by the house of their fathers, according to the number of the names, from twenty years old and upward, by their polls. (f) In showing every man his tribe and his ancestors.
