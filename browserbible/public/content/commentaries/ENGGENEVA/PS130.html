1 Length of days [is] in her right hand; {h} [and] in her left hand riches and honour. (h) Meaning, that he seeks wisdom, that is, suffers himself to be governed by the Word of God, will have all prosperity both corporal and spiritual.
3 She [is] a tree {i} of life to them that lay hold upon her: and happy [is every one] that retaineth her. (i) Which brings forth such fruit that they who eat of it have life and he alludes to the tree of life in paradise.
4 The LORD by wisdom hath {k} founded the earth; by understanding hath he established the heavens. (k) By this he shows that this wisdom of which he speaks is everlasting, because it was before all creatures and that all things even the whole world were made by it.
