1 The meek will he {g} guide in judgment: and the meek will he teach his way. (g) He will govern and comfort them that are truly humbled for their sins.
3 For thy {h} name's sake, O LORD, pardon mine iniquity; for it [is] great. (h) For no other respect.
4 What {i} man [is] he that feareth the LORD? him shall he teach in the way [that] he shall {k} choose. (i) Meaning, the number is very small. (k) He will direct such with his spirit to follow the right way.
5 His soul shall dwell at {l} ease; and his seed shall inherit the earth. (l) He will prosper both in spiritual and corporal things.
6 The {m} secret of the LORD [is] with them that fear him; and he will shew them his covenant. (m) His counsel contained in his word, by which he declares that he is the protector of the faithful.
9 The troubles of my heart {n} are enlarged: [O] bring thou me out of my distresses. (n) My grief is increased because of my enemies cruelty.
11 Consider mine {o} enemies; for they are many; and they hate me with cruel hatred. (o) The greater that his afflictions were and the more that his enemies increased, the more near he felt God's help.
