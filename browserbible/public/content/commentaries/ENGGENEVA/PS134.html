1 For they {f} sleep not, except they have done mischief; and their sleep is taken away, unless they cause [some] to fall. (f) Meaning that to do evil is more proper and natural to the wicked than to sleep, eat or drink.
2 For they eat the bread of {g} wickedness, and drink the wine of violence. (g) Gotten my wicked means and cruel oppression.
3 But the path of the just [is] as the shining light, that {h} shineth more and more unto the perfect day. (h) Signifying that the godly increase daily in knowledge and perfection, till they come to full persecution, which is when the are joined to their head in the heavens.
