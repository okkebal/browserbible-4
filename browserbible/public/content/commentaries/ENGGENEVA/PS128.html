3 For length of {a} days, and long life, and peace, shall they add to thee. (a) Long life is the blessing of God which he gives to his, so far as it is expedient for them.
4 Let not {b} mercy and truth forsake thee: bind them about thy {c} neck; write them upon the table of thine {d} heart: (b) By mercy and truth he means the commandments of the first and second table, or else the mercy and faithfulness that we ought to use toward our neighbours. (c) Keep them as a precious jewel. (d) Have them ever in remembrance.
