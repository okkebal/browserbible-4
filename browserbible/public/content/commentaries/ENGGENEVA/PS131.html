1 {l} Be not afraid of sudden fear, neither of the desolation of the wicked, when it cometh. (l) For when God destroys the wicked he will save his as he did Lot in Sodom.
3 Withhold not good from {m} them to whom it is due, when it is in the power of thine hand to do [it]. (m) Not only from them to whom the possession belongs but also you shall not keep it from them who have need of the use of it.
