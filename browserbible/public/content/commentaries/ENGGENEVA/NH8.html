2 Sallu, Amok, Hilkiah, Jedaiah. These [were] the {b} chief of the priests and of their brethren in the days of Jeshua. (b) Next in dignity to the high priests and who were of the stock of Aaron.
3 Moreover the Levites: Jeshua, Binnui, Kadmiel, Sherebiah, Judah, [and] Mattaniah, {c} [which was] over the thanksgiving, he and his brethren. (c) Had charge of them who sang the psalms.
4 Also Bakbukiah and Unni, their brethren, [were] over against them in the {d} watches. (d) They kept the wards and watches according to their turns, (2Ch 23:6).
7 And in the days of Joiakim were priests, the chief of the fathers: of {e} Seraiah, Meraiah; of Jeremiah, Hananiah; (e) That is, next to Seraiah or rather of the order, who was called after the name of Seraiah.
12 Of {f} Abijah, Zichri; of Miniamin, of Moadiah, Piltai; (f) Of which was Zachariah, John the Baptist's father.
