1 Over the nations; they shall not prevail against him, but he shall prevail over them.
2 He shall rule them; reign with Christ over all his foes.As I received of my Father; (Ps 2:8,9).
3 Give him the morning-star; to shine in glory with Christ the true Morning-star. Chap (Re 22:16).
6 A name; the merely outward profession and form of religion. Persons may be regular in the outward form of religion, and yet destitute of its spirit. Without an effectual change, such cannot escape the judgments of God.
7 Things which remain; their remaining attachment to truth and duty.
8 Received and heard; the blessings bestowed, and the truths inculcated upon them.As a thief; suddenly and unexpectedly.
9 Not defiled their garments; not embraced error or indulged in sin.In white; a state of purity and blessedness.
10 I will not blot out his name--I will confess his name before my Father, and before his angels; (Mt 10:32,33; 25:34-40). Character is personal; and amid great and abounding iniquity individuals may faithfully serve God and be ripening for glory.
