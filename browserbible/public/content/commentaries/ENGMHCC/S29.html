1 Chapter 14 Chapter Outline Joab procures Absalom's recall.(1-20) Absalom recalled.(21-24) His personal beauty.(25-27) He is admitted to his father's presence.(28-33) Verses 1-20 We may notice here, how this widow pleads God's mercy, and his clemency toward poor guilty sinners. The state of sinners is a state of banishment from God. God pardons none to the dishonour of his law and justice, nor any who are impenitent; nor to the encouragement of crimes, or the hurt of others.
