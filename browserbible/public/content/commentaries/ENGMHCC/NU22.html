1 Chapter 26 Chapter Outline Numbering of Israel in the plains of Moab.(1-51) The division of the land.(52-56) Number of the Levites.(57-62) None remaining of the first numbering.(63-65) Verses 1-51 Moses did not number the people but when God commanded him. We have here the families registered, as well as the tribes. The total was nearly the same as when numbered at mount Sinai. Notice is here taken of the children of Korah; they died not, as the children of Dathan and Abiram; they seem not to have joined even their own father in rebellion. If we partake not of the sins of sinners, we shall not partake of their plagues. (Nu 26:52-56)
