17. that planted thee--(Jr 2:21; Is 5:2).
against themselves--The sinner's sin is to his own hurt (see on Jr 7:19).


22. The retribution of their intended murder shall be in kind; just as in Messiah's case (Psa 69:8-28).


3. knowest me--(Psa 139:1).
tried ... heart--(Jr 11:20).
toward thee--rather, "with Thee," that is, entirely devoted to Thee; contrasted with the hypocrites (Jr 12:2), "near in ... mouth, and far from ... reins." This being so, how is it that I fare so ill, they so well?
pull ... out--containing the metaphor, from a "rooted tree" (Jr 12:2).
prepare--literally, "separate," or "set apart as devoted."
day of slaughter--(Jas 5:5).
