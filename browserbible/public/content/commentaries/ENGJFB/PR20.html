12. There--in the acting of violence, they are overthrown. A signal defeat.


4. desires--(Psa 20:5; 21:2), what is lawful and right, really good (Psa 84:11).


9. Two reasons: The prosperity of the wicked is short; and the pious, by humble trust, will secure all covenant blessing, denoted here by "inherit the earth" (compare Psa 25:13).


14-15. sword, and ... bow--for any instruments of violence.
slay--literally, "slaughter" (1Sa 25:11).
poor and needy--God's people (Psa 10:17; 12:5). The punishment of the wicked as drawn on themselves--often mentioned (compare Psa 7:15, 16; 35:8).


18-19. God, who knows His people's changes, provides against evil and supplies all their need.
