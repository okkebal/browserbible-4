4. roar--with bestial fury.
congregations--literally, "worshipping assemblies."
ensigns--literally, "signs"--substituted their idolatrous objects, or tokens of authority, for those articles of the temple which denoted God's presence.


9. signs--of God's presence, as altar, ark, &c. (compare Psa 74:4; 2Ch 36:18, 19; Da 5:2).
no more any prophet--(Is 3:2; Jr 40:1; 43:6).
how long--this is to last. Jeremiah's prophecy (Jr 25:11), if published, may not have been generally known or understood. To the bulk of the people, during the captivity, the occasional and local prophetical services of Jeremiah, Ezekiel, and Daniel would not make an exception to the clause, "there is no more any prophet."


13-15. Examples of the "salvation wrought" are cited.
divide the sea--that is, Red Sea.
brakest ... waters--Pharaoh and his host (compare Is 51:9, 10; Eze 29:3, 4).
