Verse 23 O love the Lord, all ye his saints - It is only the saints that can love God, as they only are made partakers of the Divine nature. Holy spirits can love God, who is the fountain of their holiness; and the saints should love him. The proud doer - The man of the proud heart, haughty and supercilious carriage, and insulting and outrageous conduct.
