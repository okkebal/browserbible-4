2 of this Psalm are generally summed up thus: The majesty and grace of Christ's kingdom; or an epithalamium of Jesus Christ and the Christian Church; the duty of this Church, and its privileges. The preamble is found in the title and Psa 45:1. The description and character of the Bridegroom, Psa 45:2-9. The address to the bride by her companions, Psa 45:10-15.
